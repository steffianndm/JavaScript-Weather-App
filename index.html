You should drink a

Kiwi Margarita.

Ingredients

  1. 2 cups whole milk
  2. 2/3 cup white chocolate chips
  3. 2 chai tea bags
  4. 1/2 cup Baileys Original Irish Cream
  5. 1 large marshmallow
  6. Freshly ground black pepper, for topping

Directions

In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until barely simmering. Add the chocolate and stir until melted. Remove from heat. Add the tea bags and let stand until infused, 4 to 5 minutes. Discard the tea bags. Reheat the hot chocolate over medium heat until just simmering. Stir in the Baileys. Divide between 2 mugs. Top with marshmallows and pepper.

You should drink a

Moscow Hot Chocolate.

Ingredients

  1. 1 fluid ounce vanilla flavored vodka
  2. 3/4 fluid ounce amaretto liqueur
  3. 1 tablespoon instant hot chocolate mix
  4. 4 fluid ounces hot milk

Directions

Into a coffee mug, pour in the vanilla vodka, amaretto, and add the hot chocolate mix. Pour in the hot milk and stir to blend well.
